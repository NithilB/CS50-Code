Yu-gi-oh

Spell


Normal: Cards that can be set in the spell & trap zone or instantly activated during your turn. Used for chains, special summoning, advancing plays etc.

Continuous: Unlike normal spells, these spells stay on the field until it's destroyed.

Quick-Play: These spells can be activated during either players turn if set on the field. This spell can be activated from the hand during any point of the battle step of your turn.

Equip: These spells target a monster on the field, and its designated effect latched onto that monster. This is usually an attack boost. This type of card still takes up a spell/trap zone.


Trap


Normal: Cards that can be set in the spell & trap zone. These cards usually harm the opponent, and can be activated during either players turn. However, trap cards can only be set, and can't be activated on the turn it is set.

Continuous: Unlike normal traps, these spells stay on the field until it's destroyed.

Counter: When spells/traps are activated their effects can't be negated or overwritten if the card is destroyed. A counter trap is usually activated after a trap or spell has been used, in order to completely negate their effects and destroy the card.
